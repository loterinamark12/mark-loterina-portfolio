# Portfolio Content Checklist

Before sharing your portfolio with recruiters:

- Add your real email address.
- Add your LinkedIn and GitHub links.
- Add live project links only when you have permission to share them.
- Add screenshots only when they do not expose private client data.
- Confirm all job dates and titles match your resume.
- Consider adding a PDF resume in the root folder and linking it from the hero section.
- After deploying, test the page on mobile and desktop.
- Run Lighthouse in Chrome DevTools and review Accessibility, Performance, SEO, and Best Practices.
